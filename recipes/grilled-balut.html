Grilled Balut

Back to main page


Description

Grilled balut is the new trend for serving the famous Filipino street food.

As the name suggests, the balut is grilled for a smoky flavor and served with chili sauce on top!

You will need:

  • Balut
  • BBQ Grill with cover (could be makeshift)
  • Metal ring (for egg support atop the grill)
  • Chili Sauce of your choosing!
  • Vinegar
  • BBQ Stick

Steps:

  1. Boil the balut and make sure it is only half-way cooked since we will grill it later on.
  2. Chop part of the egg where the air sac is. Clue: It is usually on the opposite side of the darker side.
  3. Place the metal ring atop the grill then put the egg.
  4. Put some chili sauce on the top of the balut then cover the grill. To make sure the egg gets all the smoky aroma!
  5. Poke the egg with a stick then serve it with flavored vinegar!
