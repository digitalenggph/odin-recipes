Inihaw na Isaw

Back to main page


Description

According to Tasteatlas, isaw, an inexpensive Filipino street food dish consists of boiled, then grilled pork or chicken intestines.

It is usually eaten during drinking occasions or when people go home from a long hard day-work.

You will need:

  • chicken intestines
  • soy sauce
  • vinegar
  • salt
  • peppercorn
  • dried bay leaves
  • onion
  • garlic
  • chili

Steps: (from Tasteatlas)

  1. Clean and rinse the intestines under runnning water.
  2. Turn them inside out or slice them for easier cleaning.
  3. Boil the intestines for 30 to 90 minutes
  4. Marinate them in the mixture of soy sauce, bay leaves, onions, garlic, etc.
  5. Skwer the intestines then grill them until cooked.
  6. Dip them in your favorite vinegar dip!
  7. Enjoy with friends!
